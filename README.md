# AusPac

**AusPac** is the official website for a shipping and logistics company that connects businesses and individuals with fast, reliable, and cost-effective sea and air freight solutions. This README explains what AusPac does, how our service works, and how customers or partners can contact us.

---

##  What We Do

AusPac provides end-to-end shipping and logistics services tailored to meet both commercial and personal shipping needs. We make moving goods — from small parcels to freight containers — simple, trackable, and secure.

###  Core Services

- **International Freight (Sea & Air):** Door-to-door and port-to-port shipping with multiple carrier options.  
- **Domestic Transportation:** Inland trucking and last-mile delivery for shipments within the country.  
- **Customs Clearance & Documentation:** Professional handling of export/import paperwork, duties, and compliance.  
- **Warehousing & Fulfillment:** Short- and long-term storage, inventory management, and order fulfillment.  
- **Cargo Insurance:** Optional coverage to protect goods during transit.  
- **Tracking & Customer Support:** Real-time shipment tracking and a dedicated support team.

---

##  How It Works

1. **Request a Quote:** Visit our website or contact our team with shipment details (origin, destination, weight, dimensions, and preferred service).  
2. **Choose a Service:** We provide options (economy, express, containerized, LCL/FCL, etc.) and a transparent price.  
3. **Book & Prepare:** Book the shipment, prepare documents, and pack the goods according to our packaging guidelines.  
4. **Pickup & Transit:** We arrange pickup and move the cargo via the selected transport mode.  
5. **Customs & Delivery:** We manage customs procedures and deliver the goods to the final destination.  
6. **Track & Support:** Monitor your shipment and reach our support if anything comes up.
